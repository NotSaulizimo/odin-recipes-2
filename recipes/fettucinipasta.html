Fettuccini Pasta

Description

Classic fettuccini pasta with a rich and creamy sauce.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb fettuccini pasta
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley

Steps

  1. Boil water and cook fettuccini according to package directions.
  2. Melt butter in a large pan over medium heat.
  3. Add cream and bring to a simmer.
  4. Stir in Parmesan cheese until smooth.
  5. Drain pasta and toss with sauce.
  6. Season with salt and pepper, garnish with parsley.
